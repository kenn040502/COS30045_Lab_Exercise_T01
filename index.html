Energy Smarts for Aussie Households ⚡

Welcome! This site demonstrates how appliance energy consumption can be explored in the Australian market — starting with televisions.

Why it matters

Appliances use a big share of household electricity. Choosing efficient models lowers bills and emissions.

How we’ll show it

We’ll use datasets from data.gov.au to show TV sizes, technologies, star ratings, and power use.

What’s coming

After cleaning data in KNIME (Task T01(b)), we’ll publish visualisations here under the Televisions page.
