Televisions • Energy Insights

Audience: Households buying a new TV. These visuals answer: (1) Which screen technologies are common? (2) For 55″ models, how do technologies compare on annual kWh? (3) What background do spot prices provide?

Screen Technology Mix (All Sizes)

Distribution of available TV models by technology (context for what you’ll see in stores).

Takeaway: LED/LCD usually dominates availability; OLED/QLED/Mini-LED appear in smaller shares.

Average Annual Energy – 55″ TVs by Technology

Holding size constant (55″) lets us compare technologies fairly (kWh/year).

Takeaway: Expect roughly X–Y kWh/year depending on tech; over years this affects bills.

Context: Average Spot Power Prices (2018–2024)

Background context for energy costs. Bills depend on both usage and tariff.

Takeaway: Efficient TVs reduce the “usage” component, helpful regardless of price movements.
